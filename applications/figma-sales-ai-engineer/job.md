## Role Summary

Figma is hiring a founding Sales AI Engineer to build and operationalize AI-enabled workflows across the sales organization. The role connects systems such as Salesforce, Slack, and the data stack, moves prototypes into production, measures adoption and business impact, and creates reusable patterns for broader GTM adoption.

## Core Signals To Match

- Production-grade automations and internal tools for GTM workflows.
- Salesforce and adjacent tooling integrations.
- Slack tooling, CRM-embedded experiences, or equivalent workflow surfaces.
- LLM-powered workflows using OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar platforms.
- Practical safety and quality controls: constrained outputs, checks, monitoring, and reliable execution.
- SQL and operational data fluency.
- Strong product judgment: translate ambiguous stakeholder needs into adopted tools.
- Metrics discipline: adoption, business impact, and iteration based on usage.
- Reusable templates, agents, connectors, and documentation.

## Resume Tailoring Angle

Lead with hands-on AI automation work that improved operational workflows, reduced manual effort, or increased decision quality. Prioritize bullets that show shipped internal tools, API integrations, SQL/data work, stakeholder alignment, rollout, monitoring, and measurable adoption.
